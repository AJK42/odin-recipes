Asian-Inspired Salad with Garlic Pulled Pork

Description

This Asian-Inspired Salad with Garlic Pulled Pork is quick and easy to make and full of flavor. Itâ€™s a great way to use up leftover pulled pork and get those vegetables in. It comes together in 20 minutes using a delicious homemade ginger dressing and is Whole30, gluten-free, dairy-free, and egg-free, with nut-free options.

Ingredients

  • 1-16 oz bag coleslaw
  • 2 cups leftover shredded pork
  • 1 cup mandarin orange slices
  • 1/2 cup toasted almond slivers
  • 1 small red bell pepper finely chopped
  • 3 green onions chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro

Citrus Ginger Dressing

  • 1/3 cup sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger grated
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. COMBINE all ingredients except the dressing in a large bowl. Toss well. Add dressing to taste just before serving.
  2. Alternatively, you can add ingredients to a mason jar with a lid and give it a shake until well mixed.
